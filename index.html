About Me

I am an undergraduate student in Statistics with a minor in Finance at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in Hefei, China. I expect to graduate in June 2027.

My academic interests lie at the intersection of robust optimization, stochastic modeling, decision-making under uncertainty, and quantitative finance. I am particularly interested in developing statistical and computational methods for decisions under uncertainty.

My current experience combines statistical modeling, Bayesian inference, Monte Carlo simulation, and mathematical optimization, with applications to panel data, spatial coverage systems, robust planning, and quantitative investment analysis.

Research

I study statistical and optimization methods for decision problems involving uncertainty, limited data, and operational constraints.
