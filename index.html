About

About Me

I am an undergraduate student in Statistics with a minor in Finance at the University of Science and Technology of China in Hefei, China. I expect to graduate in June 2027.

My academic interests lie at the intersection of statistical inference, stochastic modeling, optimization, and data-driven decision-making. I am particularly interested in developing statistical and computational methods for decisions under uncertainty.

My current experience spans panel-data analysis, Bayesian inference, simulation, robust optimization, spatial coverage modeling, and mathematical programming.

Research

Research Interests

I am interested in statistical and optimization methods for complex decision problems involving uncertainty, limited data, and operational constraints.

Statistical Inference

Small-sample inference, Bayesian methods, uncertainty quantification, regularized estimation, and simulation-based evaluation.

Stochastic Modeling

Probabilistic modeling and simulation for systems characterized by uncertainty, heterogeneity, and dynamic behavior.

Optimization under Uncertainty

Robust optimization, integer programming, stochastic optimization, and scalable solution methods for constrained decision problems.

Data-Driven Decision-Making

Integration of statistical learning, predictive models, and optimization for industrial, urban, and financial systems.
